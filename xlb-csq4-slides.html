The XLB Content Strategy

Building Authority & Driving Engagement with Broker Partners

Q4 2025 Campaign

The End Goal

To build trust and comfort with potential broker partners, making the decision to refer business to XL Benefits feel not just safe, but strategically brilliant.

We will achieve this by consistently showcasing our:

  • Professionalism & Service Commitment
  • Unmatched Expertise in Self-Funding
  • Proven Track Record of Results

The Broker's Journey: A 4-Phase Campaign

Our content is structured as a four-month narrative arc, guiding a broker from initial curiosity to confident partnership.

  1. Phase 1: The "Why" (September) - Highlighting the shared challenges of the fully-insured market.
  2. Phase 2: The "What" (October) - Demystifying self-funding and introducing XLB as the expert guide.
  3. Phase 3: The "How" (November) - Detailing the collaborative XLB partnership experience.
  4. Phase 4: The "Proof" (December) - Showcasing tangible results and success stories.

Content Assets: A Complete Game Plan

Our strategy utilizes two synchronized channels to surround our audience with value.

  1. LinkedIn Campaign (The Passing Game): For consistent engagement and driving traffic.
  2. Website Content Hub (The Ground Game): For deep authority, lead capture, and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO).

LinkedIn Campaign: The Passing Game

  • 30 Posts over 4 Months: A steady cadence of ~2 posts per week to build recognition and trust.
  • Follows the 4-Phase Theme: Each month's posts align with the strategic narrative.
  • Personalized CTA: Each post will drive to a sales team member's personal scheduling link, making the call to action direct and human.

Website Content Hub: The Ground Game

  • Pillar & Cluster Model: The core of our GEO strategy to build topical authority.
  • 4 Pillar White Papers: Long-form, authoritative guides that serve as cornerstone assets for lead generation.
  • 16 Cluster Blog Posts: Shorter, focused articles that explore topics from the white papers in detail, linking back to them.

Website Strategy: Building a GEO Moat

Our website content is designed to establish XLB as the definitive authority on self-funding, optimized for modern, AI-powered search engines.

We are moving beyond traditional SEO to Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), creating comprehensive, expert-driven content that directly answers the most complex questions our audience has.

The Pillar & Cluster Model

This is the core of our GEO strategy.

  • Pillar Content (The 4 White Papers): Long-form, authoritative guides that cover a major topic from top to bottom. These are our cornerstone assets.
  • Cluster Content (16 Blog Posts): Shorter, focused articles that explore a single idea from a white paper in greater detail. Each blog post links back to its parent white paper.

This structure signals deep topical authority to search engines, making our site the go-to resource for self-funding expertise.

Our Pillar Content: The White Papers

Each white paper anchors one phase of our campaign, providing a high-value asset for lead generation.

  • The True Cost of Doing Nothing: Anchors "The Why."
  • Self-Funding 101: Anchors "The What."
  • The XLB Playbook: Anchors "The How."
  • Beyond the Premium: Anchors "The Proof."

Execution: The Staggered Release

A "content dump" gets attention for a day. A strategic, staggered release builds momentum for months.

Our strategy is to create a "content heartbeat."

  • Each month, we will release one new White Paper.
  • Each week, we will publish a new supporting Blog Post.

This provides a steady stream of fresh content that aligns with our LinkedIn campaign, rewards repeat visitors, and is highly favored by search engine algorithms.

Example: Month 1 (September)

Theme: The "Why"

  • Week 1: Publish White Paper #1: "The True Cost of Doing Nothing."
  • Week 2: Publish Blog Post #1 from that cluster.
  • Week 3: Publish Blog Post #2.
  • Week 4: Publish Blog Post #3.

This cadence repeats for each of the four months, creating a powerful and sustained marketing engine.

This integrated content strategy will establish XL Benefits as the thought leader in self-funding for the broker community.
